Made in Mississippi

(where the thick-ass country accent comes from)

"Come home when the streetlights come on," my mom used to say to me and my little brother. Dirt under our nails and fresh off hours of imagination, eventually, we did.

Barefoot adventures in the Mississippi delta branded me: disciplined, instinctive, bold. But small-town dreams need sculpting. After high school, I moved to Savannah, Georgia where I finished my graphic design degree at the Savannah College of Art and Design.

"It's not what you know. It's how you Google."
— Me

Today, I live in Louisville, Kentucky and work as a UX designer at Humana. I have a keen eye for both digital and print; my background includes branding, layout, art direction, and app and website design.

Over the years, I have collaborated with developers, marketers, copywriters, clients, and photographers.
